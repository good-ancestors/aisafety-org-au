AI Safety in Australia

AI safety is the field of research and policy focused on ensuring advanced AI systems remain safe, controllable, and beneficial. This includes technical work on alignment and robustness, and governance work on the institutions and rules that shape how AI is developed and deployed.

  • AI Safety Australia & New Zealand Connect with the AI safety community in Australia and New Zealand. Events, careers, and learning.
  • Australians for AI Safety Expert advocacy for Australian government action on AI risks.
  • Good Ancestors Policy solutions for AI governance, biosecurity, and institutional reform.
  • International AI Safety Report Global scientific review of AI capabilities and risks, backed by 30+ countries.
