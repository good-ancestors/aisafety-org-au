AI Safety in Australia

Safe and ethical advanced AI could bring unprecedented benefits to humanity.

But advanced AI is not safe by default.

Australians have a crucial role to play in addressing the catastrophic risks from unsafe AI.

What is AI safety?

AI safety is the field of research and policy focused on ensuring advanced AI systems remain safe, controllable, and beneficial. This includes technical work on alignment and robustness, and governance work on the institutions and rules that shape how AI is developed and deployed.
